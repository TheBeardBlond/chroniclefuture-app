# Chronicle Future

## Mission

Help entrepreneurs, investors, operators, and forward thinkers identify
emerging opportunities and risks before they become obvious.

Chronicle Future translates local, state, national, global, technological,
economic, and geopolitical signals into actionable intelligence.

---

## Core Product

Localized Future Intelligence Platform

Users enter:

- City
- State
- ZIP

Chronicle Future generates:

### Weekly Intelligence Brief

1. Local Signals
2. State Signals
3. National Signals
4. Global Signals
5. Technology Watch
6. Commodity & Resource Watch
7. Opportunity Engine
8. Risk Radar
9. Geographic SWOT
10. Decade Outlook

- Who benefits?
- Who loses?
- What businesses become more attractive?
- What risks increase?

- Opportunity score
- Capital requirements
- Time horizon
- Confidence level
